Bank-ID
=======

Bundle for connect [Bank-ID library](https://github.com/dimafe6/bank-id) to your Symfony application.

[![license](https://img.shields.io/github/license/mashape/apistatus.svg)](LICENSE)

## Requirements

* PHP 5.6+ or 7.0+
* Symfony 2.7+ or 3+
